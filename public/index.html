In the age of the Internet, the development of an entrepreneurial activity that is willing to be meaningful, viable and oriented towards others can flourish with serenity through an approach that puts social communication and authentic conversations at the center of ones concerns. We then ask the following questions: “Why”, “For whom”, and finally “How”.
